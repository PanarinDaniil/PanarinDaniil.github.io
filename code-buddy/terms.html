1. HOW OUR SERVICES WORK

Code-Buddy offers a Marketplace for customizing websites. We connect website and business owners (“Users”) who need to update their existing websites or other coding services, with web developers (“Developers”) who are able to make these updates for Users. For example, our Site can connect Users with a Developer who can, for example, insert a logo on a website, add social networking plug-ins, or update existing content.

Users can submit a project request for website updates – herein called a “Project” – with us and request a quote for a Project by visiting https://www.Code-Buddy.com/projects/new and submitting a description of the items they would like to change. We break each Project into a series smaller projects or “Tweaks,” and provide the User a quote for completing the entire Project, based on the individual Tweaks that are needed to complete the Project. Each Tweak is allocated to a Developer for completion.

So that a Developer may Tweak your website as requested, and/or complete your Project, you must supply us with the appropriate login credentials for your website and permit Developers temporary access in order to complete your Project. Developers are not permitted to use your log-in credentials for any purposes other than to complete the Tweaks or Project. You must have the authority to authorize Code-Buddy and/or Developers to access your website in order to perform the requested services, or you may not submit a Project request.

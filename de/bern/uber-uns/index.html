About Us

ClassOwl is developed by a swiss development company and ensures that teachers and students needs are well met for everyday use in the modern classroom.

Noah Liebherr
Gründer
Alexander Degenhardt
Gründer

So one day in the school library, they started planning what their ideal EdTech solution would be like and saved up their monthly pocket money to hire a programmer to create the first version of the platform. When one of the teacher's from ZIS decided to invest in their vision, they knew that they were on the right path. A few investors and one year later, ClassOwl has brought out to market a fully-fledged LMS with interactive assignment functionalities and organisational tools to make teaching in the modern classroom less time consuming for teachers, and more engaging for students.

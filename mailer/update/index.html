Dear Forever Missed User,
I'd like to thank you once again for selecting ForeverMissed.com to commemorate the life of your loved one. It is your participation, your support, your compliments and your input that is driving us forward and helping us to improve and provide you with better experience on ForeverMissed. As some of you have already noticed, we have recently released a significant update to our system - the result of our efforts for the past three months. The most important changes will not jump out at you. Instead, you will notice that the website has become faster and more reliable. Also, a lot of the issues you've seen in the past have been resolved. Here are some highlights of this release:
Increase speed and reliability
As more and more families are starting to use the ForeverMissed.com website, its performance has become a significant issue. I am happy to say that with this upgrade the website is faster than ever.
Improved browser support
We have fixed many glitches related to the use of older browsers. The site now runs better in Internet Explorer 7, in Safari and on Macintosh.
Sponsorship plans
Many of you have expressed interest in sponsoring a memorial that was created by another member of your family or a friend. It is now possible to do so. By sponsoring, you enable everyone to upload an unlimited number of pictures, songs and video clips to the memorial. Look for the "Sponsor this Memorial" link on the right side of the page, above the visitors counter.
Various interface improvements
If you have written to our support team and pointed out an issue in the past, it is likely that we were able to resolve it with this release.
We are listening to your comments and suggestions!
And now it is much easier to make one. Just click on the "Have a Suggestion?" link in the upper right corner of your memorial and share your thoughts with us.
Warmly,
Oleg Andelman

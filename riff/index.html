The world's onlyUniversal Digital Identifier

A single number can now connect any amount of digital elements to create simple, secure and future-proof digital ecosystems around individuals, entities and things.

Unique

The 87810 number range is a unique Numbering, Naming, Addressing and Identification (NNAI) ecosystem that is both flexible and future proofed. Issued by the UN’s International Telecommunications Union (ITU), there’s nothing like it now and there is unlikely to be anything like it in the foreseeable future.

Unique Ubiquitous Universal Scalable Secure

Everyone’s digital world is expanding at warp speed. Currently, the only way to connect your diverse digital ecosystem is for third parties to hold vast amounts of your personal data, compromising both privacy and security. Without holding your data, 878TEN uses pointers to enable you to simply and securely link your entire digital ecosystem and keep control of your digital world.
